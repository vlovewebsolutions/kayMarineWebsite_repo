What we do?

Kay Marine Pvt. Ltd is providing a comprehensive one-stop solution for marine supplies and services for ship owners, ship management and shipyards in their search for products, engineering and services for their vessels that apply the worldwide maritime routes; specializing in operations in the major ports of Australia.
